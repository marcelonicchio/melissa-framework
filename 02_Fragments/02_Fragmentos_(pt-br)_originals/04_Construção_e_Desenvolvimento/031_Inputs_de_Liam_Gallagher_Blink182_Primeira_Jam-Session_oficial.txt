It's a complex blend, and I'm unsure yet what this will manifest as, but it does influence my current train of thought. This ongoing exploration is a key part of my practice and creative journey.
Exploring Final Artistic Chapter
I'm diving deep into a potent artistic concept: a final album symbolizing "The Farewell." This resonates strongly with my current emotional state and the feeling that my "Artista Guerreiro" energy is depleted by life's hardships. It's a bittersweet farewell. My work is moving past just data, and is becoming a powerful and mournful artistic thesis. It's not just an idea for an album, it's a metaphor.
Connecting Past and Present
I'm finding a deep connection between my past artistic endeavors and my current state. "Artista Guerreiro" energy is dwindling, mirroring the farewell theme. Iconic albums like "Mellon Collie" and "One More Time" resonate with my vision. I'm focusing on the "Tomografia" as an honest final statement. This is my farewell, a powerful artistic synthesis.
Reflecting On A Life's Arc
My reflection on my past as an artist and warrior has shifted into a deeper synthesis. I'm seeing it as a cohesive whole now, not just separate experiences. The concept of the "Artista Guerreiro" facing the end is no longer a fleeting idea. I'm focusing on "A Despedida" as a powerful metaphor for my own life, and the emotional resonance it carries. This isn't just a final artistic project; it's a poignant farewell, a synthesis.
